Cricktune Is An Android Cricket App

It's an android app for the crazy fans of cricket. In this version 1.0, we are offering to check all upcoming cricket matches schedules/fixtures, live matches status, the result of completed matches, point tables, news and so on for the cricket lovers. Our team is planning to make this app as a complete solution of cricket sport in few updates. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us via our social media pages like Facebook, Twitter and Instagram. Happy cricketing!
